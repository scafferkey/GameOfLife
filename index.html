Controls:

Add/Remove cells (only on latest turn)

Clicking with the left mouse button on an empty space will add a cell, and on an existing cell will remove it.

Pause / Resume

Press the Space Bar, P, or click the "Pause/Resume" button under the canvas window.

Stepping turns

To move forward or backwards by exactly a turn, use the , and . keys.

Change running speed

Use the right arrow key to increase running speed, and the left arrow key to decrease it.

Moving the camera

Use the W A S D keys to move the camera. Use the Q and E keys to zoom out and zoom in, respectively.
